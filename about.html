Conversation Filer for Outlook 2016

Conversation Filer can find other messages in the same conversation and file all of the messages in the same folder. This is particularly handy when a mailing list filing rule is broken by the Bcc feature in an attempt to take the discussion off-list. Conversation Filer can show you where the rest of the messages in the conversation were previously filed and move the new messages there with another click.

Although Conversation Filer has full read and write access to your mailbox, it does not collect any of your data or make any changes to your data other than moving items to the folder you pick. For more details, please refer to the privacy policy.
